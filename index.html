About DNS Who

Decsy Nataly Sanchez Guzman is a first-year student in Multimedia Production focused on scripting for multimedia. Decsy has always loved the shapes, colours, anime, and cartoons for that reason she started to draw. She also found inspiration in nature by doing long walks. However, as she loves the creative process and how everything can get transform from one small piece into a big one, she started to love the website building up process. One of her favourites sayings is “The things happen for a reason.” Because at the end of the road if you look back everything makes sense. Also, She has been inspired or influenced by the anime One Piece and its main character Luffy who has a leading and decisive role.
